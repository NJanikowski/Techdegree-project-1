From Codes to Coding

I grew up in the PNW and the South. at 18 I moved to Central New York and attended college there. I found that traditional schooling wasn't for me and wanted to follow a dream.

I packed my bags and moved to Central Texas with some friends. There I followed my dream of becoming a Paramedic.

Working in high volume rural 911 for 10 years. During that time I worked on several FEMA disaster deployments. As well as being in the field during the Pandemic.

With my average work week being 72 hours. I wanted a change so that I could be home more with my fiancé. I made the choice to follow another passion I had which was coding. Plus the allure of sleeping in my bed everynight and being home with family.

After hearing about Treehouse on several podcasts and many long talks with my fiancé. We decided that I should take the plunge and devot myself full time to this path. I "retired" from in field 911 and began my coding journey.

In my freetime I enjoy camping,bouldering, and hiking. As long as the weather permits. If trapped indoors then its reading,gaming, and getting a game of Warhammer 40k in.
